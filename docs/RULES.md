# CS-GWYNT Game Rules

## Overview

CS-GWYNT is an online Trading Card Game inspired by Gwent's mechanics and themes based on CentraleSupélec's associative life.

## Basic Mechanics

### Players

- Two players compete against each other
- Starting health: 20
- Starting mana: 10 per turn

### Cards

Cards have the following attributes:

- **Name**: Card identifier
- **Type**: Unit, Spell, or Artifact
- **Cost**: Mana required to play
- **Power**: Damage dealt or strength value
- **Rarity**: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary
- **Description**: Card effect/ability

### Deck

- Maximum deck size: 40 cards
- Minimum deck size: 25 cards
- Players draw from their deck each turn

### Turn Structure

1. Start of turn (draw a card)
2. Play cards and use abilities
3. Attack opponent
4. End turn

### Winning

- Reduce opponent's health to 0 or below
- Or force opponent to run out of cards

## Card Types

### Units

Combat creatures that deal damage and have health/armor values.

### Spells

One-time effects that impact the game state immediately.

### Artifacts

Persistent effects that remain on the battlefield.

## Rarity Levels

- **Common**: Basic cards, limited power
- **Uncommon**: Enhanced abilities
- **Rare**: Powerful effects
- **Legendary**: Unique, powerful cards with special abilities

## Future Expansions

- Faction system
- Special abilities (Provoke, Armor, etc.)
- Multiplayer tournaments
